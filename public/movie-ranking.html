Top 3:

Oppenheimer

The film is centered around J. Robert Oppenheimer, a key figure in the development of the atomic bomb during WWII.

Interstellar

In a dystopian future, pilot Cooper and a team journey through a wormhole near Saturn to find new habitable planets for humanity, encountering love, sacrifice, and time dilation near black holes.

Memento

A man with short-term memory loss, Leonard, uses polaroids and tattoos to hunt the man he believes killed his wife, only to grapple with the complex and manipulated truths of his own actions.
